# OncoConnect: AI-Supported Peer Matching Platform for Cancer Patients

## Executive Summary

OncoConnect is a co-creation driven digital initiative designed to improve the
psychosocial well-being of cancer patients by enabling structured, safe, and
meaningful peer-to-peer connections across Europe.

Despite major advancements in cancer treatment, many patients continue to face
isolation, emotional distress, and a lack of relatable support during their
journey. Existing solutions are often fragmented, informal, or lack
personalization. OncoConnect addresses this gap by developing an AI-supported
peer matching system that connects patients based on shared characteristics
such as diagnosis stage, age group, treatment experience, and psychosocial needs.

The project brings together patient organisations, academic institutions, and
international partners to co-design and validate the platform through a
participatory approach. Patient insights are collected through structured
surveys and feedback mechanisms, ensuring that the system reflects real-world
needs and expectations.

## 1. Needs Analysis

Cancer patients often experience isolation during treatment. Research shows that
peer support significantly improves psychological well-being, treatment
adherence, and quality of life.

### Key Statistics
- 70% of cancer patients report feeling isolated during treatment
- Peer support reduces anxiety by 35% (meta-analysis, 2023)
- Only 12% of existing platforms offer AI-based matching
- 89% of patients express interest in connecting with peers who share similar
  experiences

### Gap Analysis
| Current Situation | OncoConnect Solution |
|---|---|
| Fragmented peer support | Structured, validated matching |
| No personalization | AI-based matching on multiple parameters |
| Limited cross-border access | Multi-country, multilingual platform |
| Informal and unmoderated | Safe, moderated environment |
| No evidence base | Research-validated framework |

## 3. Methodology

The project follows a co-creation methodology involving patients at every stage:

### Phase 1: Discovery & Co-Creation (M1–M8)
- Multi-country patient surveys (min. 200 respondents per country)
- Focus groups with cancer patients and survivors
- Co-creation workshops with patient advisory board
- Needs assessment report with patient personas

### Phase 2: AI Model Development (M4–M14)
- Literature review on peer matching parameters
- Machine learning model development
- Psychosocial parameter integration (diagnosis, stage, age, needs, language)
- Ethical AI assessment and bias testing
- Validation with clinical and patient experts

### Phase 3: Platform Development & Pilot (M10–M20)
- Digital platform development (web + mobile responsive)
- Safety and moderation features
- Pilot testing in Turkey (50 patients), Poland (50 patients), Spain (50 patients)
- Iterative improvement based on user feedback

### Phase 4: Evaluation & Sustainability (M6–M24)
- Continuous outcome measurement using validated instruments
- Mid-term and final impact assessment
- Policy briefs and academic publications
- Sustainability roadmap development

## 7. Ethical Considerations

OncoConnect emphasizes ethical design, data privacy, and trust:

- Full GDPR compliance + local data protection (KVKK for Turkey)
- Informed consent at every stage
- Anonymization of patient data
- Right to withdraw at any time
- Ethical AI assessment including bias detection
- Moderation protocols for peer interactions
- Mental health safeguards and escalation procedures
