# OncoConnect – Proposal Draft

---

## Project Summary

OncoConnect is a small-scale, co-creation driven initiative designed to improve the psychosocial well-being of cancer patients through a structured peer mentorship system. The project transforms informal and fragmented peer support practices into a replicable, evidence-based, and scalable model across European contexts.

The core innovation of OncoConnect lies in introducing a structured matching protocol that pairs newly diagnosed patients with trained peer mentors based on shared characteristics such as diagnosis stage, treatment experience, age group, and psychosocial needs. This approach enhances emotional support, reduces isolation, and improves patient adaptation to illness.

The project adopts a participatory design methodology, actively involving patient organisations, academic partners, and beneficiaries in the co-creation process. Patient insights are systematically collected through surveys and feedback mechanisms, ensuring that the developed model reflects real needs.

OncoConnect produces:
- A validated peer mentorship framework  
- A structured matching protocol (AI-ready, rule-based)  
- A peer mentor training curriculum  
- A pilot implementation across three countries  
- Open-access digital tools and guidelines  

The project is intentionally designed as a small-scale, high-impact intervention aligned with Erasmus+ priorities on inclusion, adult education, and wellbeing.

The project is structured into five interconnected Work Packages ensuring efficient implementation, clear responsibilities, and measurable outputs.

**Lead:** Turkey (Coordinator)  
**Duration:** M1–M18  

Ensures overall coordination, financial management, reporting, and partner communication.

---

### WP2 – Needs Analysis & Co-Creation
**Lead:** Poland  
**Duration:** M1–M6  

Focuses on identifying psychosocial needs through surveys, stakeholder input, and patient engagement.

- Needs Analysis Report  
- Stakeholder Feedback Summary  

---

### WP3 – Matching System & Training Development
**Lead:** Spain (Universitat de Barcelona)  
**Duration:** M3–M10  

Development of the structured peer matching protocol and mentor training curriculum.

**Outputs:**
- Matching Protocol  
- Training Curriculum  
- Digital Toolkit (initial version)  

---

### WP4 – Pilot Implementation
**Lead:** Turkey  
**Duration:** M8–M15  

Real-world implementation including mentor matching, training, and mentorship cycles.

**Outputs:**
- Pilot Report  
- Participant Feedback  

---

### WP5 – Evaluation, Dissemination & Sustainability
**Lead:** Spain & Poland  
**Duration:** M1–M18  

Covers evaluation, dissemination activities, and long-term sustainability planning.

## Innovation

OncoConnect introduces a structured, measurable, and scalable peer mentorship system, transforming informal patient support into a validated methodology.

The system is designed as AI-ready, enabling future integration of advanced matching technologies without increasing complexity at this stage.

## Co-Creation Approach

A digital co-creation platform supports the project by enabling partners, patients, and stakeholders to provide structured feedback throughout the project lifecycle.

All feedback is:
- Collected systematically  
- Evaluated through defined criteria  
- Integrated into project development when relevant  

This ensures transparency, continuous improvement, and strong stakeholder engagement.
